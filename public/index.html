The People Behind the Urban Garden Dreams Podcast

Let's get to know each other. The Urban Garden Dreams Podcast is your one stop shop for all things plants. From simple tips and tricks to full in-depth guides, we have you covered when it comes to house plants!

Liam Sawyer
Owner/Producer

Liam Sawyer is a published author, hobbyist photographer, and loves to create new things such as graphic art, apps, and new clothing designs. He founded Sawyer Original in 2020 right after he published his first book Wishes of Love.

He currently attends Manhattan College for a B.S. in Computer Engineering with a Minor in Mathematics. Being the sole founder of Sawyer Original, he is constantly trying to improve the user experience for everyone who decides to visit.
